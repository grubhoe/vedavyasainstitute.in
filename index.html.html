Our Mission

Not just computers —
careers, confidence,
and futures.

Most computer centres in Jirania teach children to pass a test and get a certificate. That paper may help in one job interview — but it will not prepare your child for the world that is already here.

At Shri VedaVyasa Computer Institute, we believe every child deserves to truly understand technology — Artificial Intelligence, programming, digital safety — not as subjects to memorise, but as skills to use in real life.

Because the children who understand technology today will be the ones who create opportunities and lead tomorrow.

"The engineers and technology leaders of tomorrow are sitting in the schools of Jirania today. We are here to show them what is possible."

What We Teach

Courses designed for
the world of tomorrow

Every course we offer is chosen for one reason — it opens real doors for your child's future. Not certificates. Skills.

Computer Basics & MS Office

AI & Emerging Technology
